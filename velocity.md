# Engineering Velocity & Excellence

Project Pharos isn't just a high-performance server; it's a testament to modern software engineering practices. We track our development velocity and quality through **DORA (DevOps Research and Assessment)** metrics.

Our philosophy, rooted in Simon Sinek's "Infinite Game," ensures that our engineering processes are as sustainable as they are efficient.
